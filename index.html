Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Have more questions? Email us at hello@subtrackr.io

Is SubTrackr really free to use? +

Yes! The free plan lets you track up to 3 subscriptions with email reminders and full dashboard access. No credit card required. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited subscriptions.

How do you send reminders? +

We send email reminders 1, 3, or 7 days before a subscription renews — you choose the timing. Pro plan also includes browser push notifications.

Is my data secure and private? +

Absolutely. We never store your passwords or connect to your bank. You manually enter subscription details. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Can I import from a spreadsheet? +

Yes, Pro plan supports CSV import and export. Download our template, fill in your subscriptions, and import everything in seconds.

What's the Lifetime plan? +

The Lifetime deal gives you all Pro features forever for a one-time payment of $10. No monthly fees, no subscriptions (ironic, we know 😄). Includes all future updates.

Can I use SubTrackr for my business? +

Definitely. Pro and Lifetime plans are great for tracking SaaS tools, client subscriptions, and business software. For team/multi-user needs, contact us about enterprise pricing.
